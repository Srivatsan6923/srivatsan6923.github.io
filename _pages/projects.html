Projects

Multimodal Chest X-ray Diagnostic Assistant

Jan 2025 – May 2025 | B.Tech Thesis

Developed a modular AI agent for chest X-ray interpretation using GPT-4o-mini, integrating segmentation, classification, grounding, VQA, and report generation, supported by a RAG-enabled vector database for retrieval-augmented reasoning.

Achieved transparent and safe diagnostic support with human-in-the-loop feedback and benchmarked performance on ChestAgentBench and CheXBench datasets.

Defect-Aware Denoising and Deblurring Network

Oct 2024 – Nov 2024 | EE5179-Deep Learning for Imaging (KLA Project)

Built an enhanced U-Net with Spatial-Channel Attention and Non-Local Blocks for industrial defect image restoration. Trained on MVTec AD using composite loss functions, achieving 30.55 dB PSNR and 0.8450 SSIM with strong defect preservation.

Comparative Evaluation of Z-axis 3D Super-Resolution Techniques

Aug 2024 – Sept 2024 | MiRL Lab, IIT Madras

Comparative study of z-axis 3D MRI super-resolution using Soup-GAN (GAN), DisC-Diff (Diffusion), and CuNeRF (Implicit Fields). Evaluated on IXI T1-weighted dataset with DisC-Diff offering best fidelity despite minor hallucinations.

Analyzed trade-offs between image quality and inference speed, recommending hybrid frameworks combining generative and implicit methods for better clinical deployment.
